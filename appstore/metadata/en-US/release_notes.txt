Welcome to HealthSync Helper App!

Securely export your Apple Health data to your Mac over local WiFi — no cloud, no accounts.

What's included:
- 31 health data types across 5 categories
- QR code pairing — no passwords needed
- TLS 1.3 encryption on every transfer
- CSV and JSON export formats
- macOS CLI companion via Homebrew
- Complete audit log of all data access
- Open source under Apache 2.0
