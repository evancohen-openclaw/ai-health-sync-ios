Your health data belongs on your devices — not on someone else's server.

HealthSync Helper App transfers Apple Health data directly from iPhone to Mac over your local WiFi. No cloud. No accounts. No tracking. No subscriptions.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Open the app and tap Start Sharing
2. Scan the QR code with the macOS companion
3. Export your health data in CSV or JSON

Three steps. Your data never leaves your network.

WHAT YOU CAN EXPORT

31 health data types across 5 categories:

Activity — Steps, walking + running distance, cycling distance, active energy, basal energy, exercise time, stand hours, flights climbed, workouts
Heart — Heart rate, resting heart rate, walking HR average, HRV, blood pressure (systolic/diastolic), blood oxygen, respiratory rate, body temperature, VO2 max
Sleep — Sleep analysis, time in bed, time asleep, time awake, REM sleep, core sleep, deep sleep
Body — Weight, height, BMI, body fat percentage, lean body mass

SECURITY YOU CAN VERIFY

Every transfer uses TLS 1.3 encryption with certificate fingerprint pinning. Pairing happens through a short-lived QR code — no passwords, no accounts. All secrets live in the iOS Keychain. A full audit log records every data access. Rate limiting protects against abuse.

The source code is open under Apache 2.0. You can read every line on GitHub.

TOTAL PRIVACY

Zero analytics. Zero third-party SDKs. Zero advertising libraries. Zero crash reporters. Zero cloud storage. Zero data collection.

Your health data travels from your iPhone to your Mac and nowhere else.

macOS COMPANION

Install the healthsync CLI via Homebrew to receive data on your Mac:
brew tap mneves75/tap && brew install healthsync

Discover devices, pair via QR code, fetch data, check status — all from Terminal. Export to CSV for spreadsheets or JSON for automation.
